# Privacy policy

Below is an outline of Audiopaste's practices regarding cookies and data
retention.

## Use of cookies

Audiopaste uses a cookie to identify your browser between visits. This
cookie allows us to:

* Identify which user uploaded each audio paste.
* Provide you with a personalized experience, including showing you a
  list of all your own pastes.

### Key Details About the Cookie:

* The cookie is a random string.
* It does not contain any personal information about you or your
  browser or computer.
* The cookie is not displayed when viewing or sharing a paste.

## Data Retention and Deletion

* Pastes are soft-deleted by default after 30 days.
* Soft-deleted pastes are periodically purged to ensure data is not
  retained longer than necessary.

## Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (such as GDPR), you may have the
following rights:

* The right to access the data associated with your account or pastes.
* The right to request the deletion of your data.
* The right to restrict or object to the processing of your data.
